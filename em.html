Easy Maintenance

Anyone who rents a house or apartment needs to report home maintenance issues. Most communities ask renters to report issues via phone call or report in person. But there are many limitations in these traditional approaches. To make renters' life easier, I designed a mobile app to easily report maintenance issues, check the status of an order, and leave reviews for the service.

My Role

This is an individual project and I did user research, prototyping, UI design, animation, and documentation.

Discover the Problems

As a tenant, I have experienced many inconveniences on maintenance issues reporting. But I need to get a larger picture of what renters have experienced. So I designed an online survey and sent it to 10 friends I know who are living in a rental place in the US.

Here's a quick digest of the research results:

01. Maintenance Issues are Inevitable

All participants reported at least 1 maintenance issues in the past 6 months. 20% reported more than 3 issues.

02. Mostly are Common Issues

People experience similar maintenance issues. None had urgent issues.

03. Major Pain Points

“Business time limitation.”
“You need to wait for reply.”
“They sometimes come in while I’m not at home and think they have fixed an issue but have not.”
“Maybe language issues.”

Project Goals

01. Help users save time and energy

Maintenance issues are frustrating, but reporting maintenance issues shouldn't be. Within just a few clicks and the maintenance people will show up at the preferred time.

02. Make users feel informed and valued

Users can check real-time order status in the app and resubmit orders if unsatisfied with previous work.

03. Get people connected with the community

Get residents involved by allowing them to rate the experience and leave comments.

User Stories

User Story 1

As an resident, I want to be able to report maintenance issues quickly, anytime and anywhere, so that I can save time and energy.

User Story 2

As a resident, I want to track the status of my request, so that I can know if the maintenance has come when I was not at home.

User Story 3

As a resident, I want to rate the experience of home maintenance, so that I can help them improve the service and get better experience in the future.
